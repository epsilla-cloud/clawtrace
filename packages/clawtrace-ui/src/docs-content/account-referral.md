# Account and Referrals

Manage your profile, sign out, and invite friends to earn free credits.


![Account and Referral](/docs/images/4-account-and-referral.png)
## Profile

Your account page shows your name, email, and avatar from your OAuth provider (Google or GitHub). Click **Sign Out** to end your session.

## Referral Program

Invite friends to ClawTrace and both of you earn **100 free credits** each.

### How It Works

1. Copy your unique referral link from the Account page
2. Share it with a friend
3. When they sign up using your link, you both receive 100 credits
4. Credits appear in your billing history as "Referral Reward" (for you) and "Invited Bonus" (for your friend)

### Referral Link

Your referral link looks like:

```
https://www.clawtrace.ai/login?invitecode=XXXXXXXX
```

Click the **copy button** next to the link to copy it to your clipboard.


### My Referrals

The table at the bottom of the Account page shows everyone who signed up using your link, along with when they joined.

### Referral Limits

- Maximum **50 referrals per month** per user
- Referral credits expire after **1 year**
- Self-referrals are not allowed

## Sign In Options

ClawTrace supports sign-in with:

- **Google** — OAuth 2.0 with email and profile access
- **GitHub** — OAuth with user and email access

Each provider creates a separate ClawTrace account tied to that provider's identity.
